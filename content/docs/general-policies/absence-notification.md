# Absence Notification Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes procedures for notifying supervisors and team members about absences from work.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees of Adapt Psychiatry.

## Procedures

### Planned Absences

1. Submit time-off requests at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Obtain approval from your direct supervisor
3. Update your calendar and notify relevant team members

### Unplanned Absences

1. Notify your supervisor as soon as possible
2. Call the main office line: (716) 929-6888
3. Leave a voicemail if no one answers
4. Follow up with an email

### Emergency Absences

In case of emergency:
- Contact your supervisor within 24 hours
- Provide documentation when available
- Coordinate with HR for extended absences

## Contact Information

- **Main Office:** (716) 929-6888
- **HR Email:** hr@adaptwny.com
- **After Hours:** Follow the emergency contact protocol

## Documentation Requirements

Absences exceeding 3 consecutive days require:
- Medical documentation (if applicable)
- Completed leave request form
- Supervisor approval

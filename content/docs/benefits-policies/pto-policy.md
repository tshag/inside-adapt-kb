# Paid Time Off Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for paid time off (PTO) according to the following schedule:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days |
| 3-5 years | 20 days |
| 6+ years | 25 days |

## PTO Accrual

- PTO accrues bi-weekly with each pay period
- New employees begin accruing immediately
- Part-time employees accrue prorated PTO

## Requesting PTO

### Advance Notice Requirements

- **1-2 days:** 1 week notice
- **3-5 days:** 2 weeks notice
- **1+ weeks:** 1 month notice

### How to Request

1. Submit request through the HR portal
2. Notify your direct supervisor
3. Update team calendar
4. Arrange coverage if needed

## Carryover Policy

- Up to 5 days may be carried over to the next calendar year
- Carryover days must be used by March 31
- Excess PTO is forfeited

## Holiday Schedule

Adapt Psychiatry observes the following paid holidays:

- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

## Sick Leave

- 5 paid sick days per year (separate from PTO)
- Doctor's notes required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick days do not carry over

## Family and Medical Leave

Eligible employees may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid FMLA leave for:
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Serious health condition
- Care for a family member with serious health condition

Contact HR for FMLA eligibility requirements and application process.

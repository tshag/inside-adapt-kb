## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible for paid time off (PTO) according to the following schedule:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days | Accrual Rate (per pay period) |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------------------------|
| 0-2 years | 15 days (120 hours) | 4.62 hours |
| 3-5 years | 20 days (160 hours) | 6.15 hours |
| 6+ years | 25 days (200 hours) | 7.69 hours |

## PTO Accrual

### Accrual Details

- PTO accrues bi-weekly with each pay period (26 pay periods per year)
- New employees begin accruing immediately upon hire
- Part-time employees accrue prorated PTO based on scheduled hours
- PTO is available for use as it accrues (no waiting period)

### Maximum Accrual

- Employees may accrue up to 1.5x their annual PTO allotment
- Once maximum is reached, accrual pauses until balance decreases
- Employees receive notification when approaching maximum

## Requesting PTO

### Advance Notice Requirements

| Duration of PTO | Notice Required |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1-2 days | 1 week |
| 3-5 days | 2 weeks |
| 1+ weeks | 1 month |
| Holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas) | 2 months |

### How to Request

1. **Submit request through the HR portal**
   - Log into ADP Workforce Now
   - Navigate to "Time & Attendance"
   - Select "Request Time Off"

2. **Notify your direct supervisor**
   - Send email with dates and coverage plan
   - Include any urgent items requiring attention

3. **Update team calendar**
   - Add to shared Outlook calendar
   - Include backup contact information

4. **Arrange coverage if needed**
   - Coordinate with colleagues for patient coverage
   - Update voicemail and email auto-reply
   - Ensure urgent messages reach appropriate person

### Approval Process

- Supervisor must approve all PTO requests
- Requests should be responded to within 48 hours
- If denied, supervisor must provide alternative dates
- Emergencies may be approved retroactively

## Carryover Policy

### Annual Carryover

- Up to **5 days** (40 hours) may be carried over to the next calendar year
- Carryover days must be used by **March 31**
- Excess PTO is forfeited on April 1
- No payout for forfeited PTO

### PTO Payout on Separation

- Accrued, unused PTO is paid out upon termination
- Calculated at current hourly rate
- Paid on final paycheck
- Maximum payout: 1x annual allotment

## Sick Leave

### Sick Leave Policy

- **5 paid sick days** per year (separate from PTO)
- Accrues at 1.54 hours per pay period
- Doctor's notes required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**
- Unused sick days do not carry over
- Sick leave is not paid out upon separation

### Using Sick Leave

- Notify supervisor as soon as possible
- Call main office: (716) 929-6888
- Email: supervisor@adaptwny.com
- Update team on expected return date

## Family and Medical Leave (FMLA)

### Eligibility

- Employed for at least 12 months
- Worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months

### Leave Entitlement

Eligible employees may take up to **12 weeks** of unpaid FMLA leave for:

- Birth or adoption of a child
- Serious health condition (employee)
- Care for family member with serious health condition
- Qualifying exigency related to military service

### Process

1. Contact HR at least 30 days in advance (when possible)
2. Complete FMLA request form
3. Provide required documentation
4. HR will approve/deny within 5 business days

Contact HR for FMLA eligibility requirements and application process.

## PTO During Probation

- New employees accrue PTO during 90-day probation
- PTO may be used during probation with supervisor approval
- Excessive PTO use during probation may extend probation period

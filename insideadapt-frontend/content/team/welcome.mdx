## Welcome to Adapt Psychiatry!

We're thrilled to have you join our team. This guide will help you get oriented and set up for success in your new role.

## Your First Day

### Before You Arrive

- [ ] Complete new hire paperwork (sent via email)
- [ ] Bring two forms of ID for I-9 verification
- [ ] Review the Employee Handbook
- [ ] Prepare any questions you have

## Getting Set Up

### IT Accounts

Your IT accounts will be created before your start date:

- **Google Workspace** (email, calendar, drive)
- **EHR System** (patient records)
- **Telehealth Platform** (virtual sessions)
- **VPN Access** (remote work)

**Login credentials** will be provided by IT on your first day.

### Workspace

Your workspace includes:
- Laptop or desktop computer
- Phone (if applicable)
- Access card/key
- Name badge
- Basic office supplies

### Parking

- Employee parking is available behind the building
- Your parking spot number will be assigned by HR
- Visitor parking is in the front lot

## Your First Week

### Training Schedule

**Day 1-2: Orientation**
- Company overview and culture
- Policy review
- System training
- HIPAA compliance

**Day 3-5: Role-Specific Training**
- Shadow experienced team members
- Practice with EHR system
- Learn workflows and procedures
- Meet key stakeholders

### Key Resources to Review

1. **Employee Handbook** - Company policies and procedures
2. **HIPAA Training Materials** - Privacy and security requirements
3. **Clinical Protocols** - Treatment guidelines (clinical staff)
4. **Benefits Guide** - Health insurance, PTO, etc.

## Company Culture

### Our Values

1. **Patient-Centered Care** - Everything we do is for our patients
2. **Excellence** - We strive for the highest quality in all we do
3. **Collaboration** - We work together as a team
4. **Innovation** - We embrace new ideas and approaches
5. **Integrity** - We act with honesty and professionalism

### Communication

- **Email** - Primary communication method
- **Slack** - Team messaging (invitation sent by IT)
- **Staff Meetings** - Weekly all-hands (Fridays at 9 AM)
- **Department Meetings** - Varies by team

### Dress Code

- **Business casual** is standard
- **Clinical staff** - Professional attire appropriate for patient care
- **No jeans** except on designated casual days
- **Name badges** must be worn at all times

## Benefits Overview

### Health Insurance

- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Coverage begins first of month after hire date
- Open enrollment annually in November

### Paid Time Off

- 15 days PTO (years 0-2)
- 5 sick days annually
- 6 paid holidays
- See PTO Policy for details

### Additional Benefits

- 401(k) with company match (after 1 year)
- Continuing education stipend
- Professional liability insurance
- Employee assistance program

## Clinical Staff Specifics

### Credentialing

- Ensure all licenses are current
- Complete payer credentialing applications
- Maintain malpractice insurance
- Update CAQH profile regularly

### Scheduling

- Patient sessions scheduled through EHR
- Templates set by Clinical Director
- Notify scheduler of any changes 48 hours in advance

### Documentation

- All notes completed within 24 hours
- Use approved templates and protocols
- Ensure HIPAA compliance in all documentation

## Administrative Staff Specifics

### Office Hours

- Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Lunch: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM (flexible)
- Core hours: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

### Phone Coverage

- Main line answered during business hours
- Backup coverage when primary is unavailable
- Voicemail checked every hour
